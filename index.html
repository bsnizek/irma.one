My research interests

Cartographic Design

ï»¿Cartography is a unique mixture of science, art and technology with necessity of variety of in-depth knowledge and skills. Cartographic design is a complex process of data visualization that requires intellectual and graphical design.

Usability studies

Usability evaluation is a combination of multiple components associated with learnability, efficiency, memorability, errors and satisfaction. It is one of the main reference in cartography and intends to support the user in achieving desirable map design through several measurement aspects.

Spatio-temporal analysis

Spatio-temporal analysis is a way of exploration of large streams of movement data collected through tracking devises. Different domains are using it to reveal useful information from complex movement patterns. In traffic network, spatio-temporal analysis is used to extract knowledge on whereabouts of traffic patterns that exists at a certain time and location.

GeoVisual Analytics

Current visualization solutions often fail to analyse large streams of movement dataset. They require a new approach towards geovisual analytics and exploratory tools. Alternative representations have to be created or the functionality of existing ones extended. They have to be judged through usability research.
